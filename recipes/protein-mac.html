Protein Mac & Cheese

Description

Protein mac is a quick and easy way of making mac and cheese to be mostly healthy and full of protein

Ingredients

  • Macaroni product(Elbow preferably)
  • Cottage cheese 450oz
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Combine Cottage cheese and spices into blender
  2. Cook pasta
  3. On low heat, in seperate pan add cottage cheese blend to pan and mix cheese
  4. Combine Cheese and Macaroni product
